# Pyro Logger

Weekly Pyrotechnic Work Experience Log for the Joint Industry SFX Grading Scheme.

## Install on Your Phone

### iPhone (Safari)
1. Open https://thegdyne.github.io/pyro-logger/ in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

## How to Use

1. Fill in your details (name, grade, production info)
2. Check the days you worked
3. Describe the effects and your involvement
4. Tap **Generate PDF** to create the official form
5. Share or save the PDF

Your entries are saved automatically and appear at the bottom of the app.
