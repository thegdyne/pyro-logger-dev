# Pyro Logger

Weekly Pyrotechnic Work Experience Log for the Joint Industry SFX Grading Scheme.

## Install on Your Phone

### iPhone (Safari)
1. Open https://thegdyne.github.io/pyro-logger/ in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

## How to Use

1. Fill in your details (name, grade, production info)
2. Check the days you worked
3. Describe the effects and your involvement
4. Tap **Generate PDF** to create the official form
5. Share or save the PDF

Your entries are saved automatically and appear at the bottom of the app.

## Privacy

We collect anonymous installation data to understand how many people use the app. **No personal information is collected.**

Here's exactly what we see when someone installs:

| Field | Example Value | What it is |
|-------|---------------|------------|
| device_id | `dev_th40wxwe6mjrk` | Random string generated by your browser |
| installed_at | `2025-12-29T19:55:23Z` | When you installed |
| device_type | `iOS` | Just the platform (iOS/Android) |
| app_version | `1.0.0` | Which version you installed |
| source | `standalone` | How the install was detected |

That's it. No names, no email, no location, no tracking across sites. We just count installs.
